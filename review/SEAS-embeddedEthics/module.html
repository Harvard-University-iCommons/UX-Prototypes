Embedded EthicCS @ Harvard: bringing ethical reasoning into the computer science curriculum, because ethics and social responsibility are part of the computer scientist’s work.

Repository of Open Source Course Modules

The Embedded EthiCS approach adds short ethics modules to computer science courses in the core computer science curriculum. By embedding ethics broadly across the curriculum, this approach meets three goals for computer science students:

Ethical sciences

The extent to which ethical issues permeate almost all areas of computer science.

Ethical industry

The variety of concrete ethical issues and problems that arise across the field.

Ethical experience

The repeated experiences of reasoning through those issues and communicating their positions effectively.
