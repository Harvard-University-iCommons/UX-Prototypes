Embedded EthiCS @ Harvard: bringing ethical reasoning into the computer science curriculum.

Repository of Open Source Course Modules

The Embedded EthiCS approach adds short ethics modules to computer science courses in the core computer science curriculum.

Ethical sciences

The extent to which ethical issues permeate almost all areas of computer science.

Ethical industry

The variety of concrete ethical issues and problems that arise across the field.

Ethical experience

The repeated experiences of reasoning through those issues and communicating their positions effectively.
