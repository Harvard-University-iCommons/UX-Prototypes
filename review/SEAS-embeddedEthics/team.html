Embedded EthiCS @ Harvard: bringing ethical reasoning into the computer science curriculum.

Meet the Team

Embedded EthiCS is a collaboration between philosophers and computer sciences at all levels. It is organized by a team of faculty from both fields. It is taught by advanced philosophy graduate students alongside computer science faculty. And our post-doctoral fellows run a week Teaching Lab for advanced graduate students in both fields to develop course modules.
