Embedded EthiCS @ Harvard: bringing ethical reasoning into the computer science curriculum.

Meet the Team

Embedded EthiCS is a collaboration between philosophers and computer scientists led by faculty from both fields. Post-doctoral fellows and graduate fellows meet weekly for a teaching lab to develop Embedded EthiCS modules.
