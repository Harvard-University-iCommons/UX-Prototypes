Embedded ethics at Harvard: Harvard: bringing ethical reasoning into the computer science curriculum.

Ethical reasoning

Ethical reasoning is an essential skill for today’s computer scientists. Embedded EthiCS teaches students how to think through the ethical and social implications of their work. Our distributed pedagogy embeds philosophers directly into courses across the standard computer science curriculum to teach a module on an ethical issue raised by the course material.
